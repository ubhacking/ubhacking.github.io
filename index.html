Frequently Asked Questions

Will there be travel reimbursement available?

Yes there will be! We are planning on offering at least $100 in travel reimbursement. Additionally, if you can get enough interest from students at your school, we will be able to send a bus!

Do I need programming experience to come?

Not at all! Hackathons are a great opportunity to learn programming in a supportive enviornment!

Are grad students allowed?

Of course! Any undergrad or grad student is welcome!

How long is it?

It will be 24 hours of hacking from 1pm on Nov. 14 until 1pm on Nov. 15. A full schedule for the event will be made public shortly!

What size team am I allowed to have?

We are currently capping team size at 4 members. However, you can be on a team of a smaller size.

What should I bring?

You will of course need to bring your laptop and charger. On top of that, you should probably bring a phone charger. A power strip and a refillable water bottle should be on your list as well. Any hardware you plan to hack with should be brought, and maybe a sweatshirt if you happen to get cold.
